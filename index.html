Why the Atmosphere Matters

Earth's atmosphere is a delicate, dynamic system of gases that protects us from harmful solar radiation, regulates temperature, and provides the oxygen we breathe. It is divided into five main layers, each with unique characteristics.

Troposphere

0 – 12 km
Where weather happens • Contains ~80% of atmosphere's mass

Stratosphere

12 – 50 km
Ozone layer • Temperature increases with altitude

Mesosphere

50 – 85 km
Meteors burn up here • Coldest layer
