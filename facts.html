Layers of Earth's Atmosphere

Interesting Facts About Each Layer

Troposphere Stratosphere Mesosphere Thermosphere Exosphere

Troposphere

  • Lowest layer of the atmosphere.
  • Where weather happens (rain, snow, storms).
  • Contains about 75% of the atmosphere’s mass.
  • Airplanes usually fly near the top of this layer.

Stratosphere

  • Contains the ozone layer.
  • Ozone absorbs harmful UV radiation from the Sun.
  • Commercial jets often fly in the lower stratosphere.

Mesosphere

  • Meteors burn up in this layer.
  • Coldest layer of the atmosphere.
  • Temperatures can drop to -90°C (-130°F).

Thermosphere

  • Very high temperatures due to solar radiation.
  • Auroras occur in this layer.
  • The International Space Station orbits here.

Exosphere

  • Outermost layer of the atmosphere.
  • Gradually fades into outer space.
  • Contains very few gas particles.
